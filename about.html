WHO WE ARE...

Established in 2022, Yes, and Where? is a collective of veteran improvisers, dedicted to support aspiring improvisers in taking their skills to the next level. Whether you are looking to find a 101 to start your journey or a seasoned improverser ready to shake it up with new curriculum from accross the nation.

WHAT WE DO...

Each member of our team is ready to help you find the best imoprov theater for your specific skill level and personal preferences of learning styles. We are here to build an improv journey that's right for you!

Mission Statement

Yes, and Where? is devoted to helping improvisers find safe, thriving community where performers of all levels can hone their craft and play an active role in growing world of improv.
